Customer-facing output

Intake Summary

Water Loss

  • When did you first notice the loss?
  • How much water are you adding, and how often?
  • Does the loss change when the pump is on or off?
  • Does the level stop near a skimmer, light, return, step, tile line, or crack?
  • Is an autofill running more than usual?

Equipment

  • Is water visible around the pump, filter, heater, valves, unions, or waste line?
  • Has filter pressure changed recently?
  • Does the pump lose prime or struggle to start?
  • Are air bubbles coming from the return jets?
  • Has anyone changed valves, timers, automation, or pump speed?

Safety And Context

  • Have any breakers or GFCI outlets tripped?
  • Has anyone felt tingling in or around the pool?
  • Is there water near lights, junction boxes, outlets, or automation?
  • Was there recent resurfacing, plumbing, equipment, deck work, storm damage, or heavy use?
  • What worries you most: water loss, damage, safety, cost, or uncertainty?
